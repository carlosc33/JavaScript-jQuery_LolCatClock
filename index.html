A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in the providence of Dallas. My journey into tech started with a degree in political science and a minor in management earned through the University of North Texas at Denton. As I started sharing my writing online, I was fascinated with how easily I could get my work out there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to programming and development, where I can use my organizational skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
